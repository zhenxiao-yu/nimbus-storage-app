# Welcome to Nimbus

Hi there — and thanks for signing up.

Nimbus is your personal cloud: a calm, fast place to keep documents, images,
and media without the noise of a full-blown drive product. Think of it as a
quiet sky for the files that matter — violet to indigo to a thin sliver of
morning sky.

## A quick tour

- **Upload** anything via the top-right uploader, or drag a file onto the page.
- **Organize** by type from the sidebar: Documents, Images, Media, Others.
- **Preview** without leaving the page — click any file card.
- **Share** by email from the file's menu; recipients see it in their dashboard.
- **Trash** is a soft delete. Restore from the Trash view any time.

## A few small things to know

- Your free workspace is 2 GB. The usage ring on the dashboard tracks it.
- Search and sort live in the URL, so links you copy reproduce the same view.
- Dark mode follows your system by default — toggle from the header.

## What's next

This file you're reading is the only thing in your workspace right now. Delete
it whenever you'd like — Nimbus won't put it back. Then upload something real
and watch the cloud fill up.

Welcome aboard.

— The Nimbus team
